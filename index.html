About Me

I specialize in leading cross-functional teams on cross-industry AI projects, focusing on building and deploying robust machine learning and deep learning models. My experience includes implementing innovative solutions for scalable data and integrating best practices to prioritize usability and feasibility. Proficient in tools like TensorFlow and PyTorch, I excel in managing large-scale datasets, ensuring data quality, and utilizing Git for version control. I am dedicated to delivering actionable insights that align with product strategy and drive process improvements.
